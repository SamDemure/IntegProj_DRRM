Accident

Accidents encompass a wide range of unexpected events resulting in unintended harm, injury, or damage. These incidents can occur in various settings, from daily life activities to workplace environments, on the road, or during recreational pursuits.

Accidents, while unpredictable, can often be prevented or their impact minimized through proactive measures, proper training, and swift, appropriate responses when they occur. Understanding the context and taking preventive steps are crucial for accident mitigation and ensuring safety in various environments.
