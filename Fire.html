Fire

Fire is a chemical reaction that releases light and heat. It occurs when a combustible material combines with oxygen in the presence of heat, resulting in a flame or glow.

The fire triangle is a simple representation of the elements necessary for fire: fuel, heat, and oxygen. These three components must be present simultaneously for a fire to occur.

Understanding fire, its elements, and classifications is essential for fire safety, prevention, and effective response in case of a fire emergency.
