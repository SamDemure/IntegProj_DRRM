Elements of Fire

Fuel

This is the material that undergoes combustion. It can be solid, liquid, or gas. Common examples include wood, paper, gasoline, and natural gas.

Heat

Fire needs an initial heat source to start the combustion process. It provides the energy required to initiate and sustain the chemical reaction.

Oxygen

The presence of oxygen in the air is crucial for fire. It's one of the essential elements that sustains the combustion process.
