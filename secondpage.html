Typically, earthquakes are reported by its magnitude, which characterizes the relative size of the earthquake. Based on their magnitude, earthquakes, are classified as follows.

MICRO

Magnitude less than 3.0

MINOR

Magnitude 3.0 - 3.9

LIGHT

Magnitude 4.0 - 4.9

MODERATE

Magnitude 5.0 - 5.9

STRONG

Magnitude 6.0 - 6.9

MAJOR

Magnitude 7.0 - 7.9

GREAT

Magnitude greater than 8.0
