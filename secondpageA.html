Causes

Accidents may result from human error, equipment failure, unsafe conditions, lack of proper training, negligence, or unforeseen circumstances.

Impact

Accidents can cause physical injuries, emotional trauma, property damage, financial loss, and even loss of life. Their effects can extend to individuals, families, communities, and businesses.

Prevention

Many accidents are preventable through proper safety measures, risk assessments, training, and adherence to safety protocols.

Types of Accident

Accidents can involve slips, falls, collisions, electrical incidents, chemical spills, machinery malfunctions, and more, depending on the setting and circumstances.
